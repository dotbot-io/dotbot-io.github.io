DotBot: the Open Didatic Robot

DotBot combines open-source hardware and rapid prototyping techniques with an online community of teachers and makers.

Follow us on Facebook

DotBot was born in 2016 thanks to the effort of Michele Maffucci (mainly) and Ludovico Russo!

The main goal of the project is to design and develop a general teaching platform to spread educational robotics.

This page is under contraction, we will add soon information about the project, but you can find lots of material on the Michele Maffucci persona site (sorry, it's in Italian), and on Thingiverse.
